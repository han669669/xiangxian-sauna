Our Journey / 启发点

A Heartwarming Tale

Once a quaint corner in the bustling city, our traditional Chinese medicine sauna and foot soak therapy shop is a sanctuary for those seeking solace and healing.

In the winter of 2020, we opened our doors, offering a blend of ancestral practices and a peaceful retreat in the heart of the community.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that wellness arises from harmony within. We combine age-old traditions with a touch of modern convenience, ensuring every experience is both authentic and accessible to our visitors.

With each passing season, our bond with the community grows stronger, as we continue to provide a tranquil space for relaxation and rejuvenation.

Join us in this serene haven and embrace the balance of body and spirit.

我们的故事

在繁忙的城市中，我们为寻找安慰和治疗的人们提供了一个避风港。2020年冬天，我们开始了这段旅程，将祖传的智慧与社区的宁静相融合。

随着季节的更迭，我们与社区的联系也愈发紧密，我们持续提供一个放松和恢复活力的宁静空间。

在这个宁静的避风港，与我们一起体验身心的平衡与和谐。
